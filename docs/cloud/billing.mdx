## Trace Limits and Overage

Each tier includes a monthly trace allowance:

| Tier | Monthly Traces | Limit Type |
|------|:--------------:|:----------:|
| **Monitor** | 5,000 | Hard limit |
| **Protect** | 25,000 | Soft limit |
| **Enforce** | 100,000 | Soft limit |
| **Govern** | 500,000 | Soft limit |

- **Hard limit** (Monitor): Traces beyond the limit are dropped. Upgrade to continue capturing.
- **Soft limit** (Protect, Enforce, Govern): Traces beyond the included allowance are billed as overage at **$0.20 per trace**.

Overage charges appear on the next monthly invoice. You can set overage alerts and hard caps at **Settings > Billing > Usage Alerts** to prevent unexpected charges.

## Billing Portal

Access billing at **Settings > Billing**:

- View current plan and usage
- Update payment method
- Download invoices
- Manage seats
- Upgrade/downgrade plan

## Upgrade

### Via Dashboard

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select new tier
4. Confirm changes
5. New features activate immediately

### Proration

When upgrading mid-cycle:
- Charged pro-rated amount for remaining days
- New tier features activate immediately
- Next invoice reflects new plan

## Downgrade

### Via Dashboard

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select lower tier
4. Review feature changes
5. Confirm downgrade

### Effective Date

Downgrades take effect at end of current billing cycle:
- Keep current features until cycle ends
- Data beyond new retention limit is archived
- Managed endpoints above limit are suspended

## Seats

### Add Seats

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Seats**
2. Click **Add Seats**
3. Enter number of additional seats
4. Confirm purchase

Seat additions are pro-rated for the current billing cycle.

### Remove Seats

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Seats**
2. Click **Manage Seats**
3. Select seats to remove
4. Confirm removal

Seat removals take effect at end of billing cycle.

## Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods:
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (US only)
- Wire transfer (Govern tier)

### Update Payment Method

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Payment**
2. Click **Update Payment Method**
3. Enter new payment details
4. Confirm

## Invoices

Download invoices at **Settings > Billing > Invoices**:

- Monthly invoices available for paid tiers
- PDF format with line-item details
- Customizable billing details (company name, address, tax ID)

### Invoice Details

Customize invoice details:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Details**
2. Enter company information
3. Add tax ID if applicable
4. Save changes

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

### After Cancellation

- Access continues until end of billing cycle
- Data retained for 30 days after expiration
- Can reactivate within 30 days without data loss
- After 30 days, data is permanently deleted
